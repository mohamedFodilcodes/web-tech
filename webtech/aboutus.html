Interesting Facts About BMW

Founding Date

BMW was founded on March 7, 1916. It originally focused on aircraft engines.

The Logo

The BMW logo is often thought to represent a spinning propeller, linking back to the company's aviation origins.

Innovation in Automotive

BMW is a pioneer in automotive technology, being the first to introduce technologies like head-up displays and active suspension systems.
